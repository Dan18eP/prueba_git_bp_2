MY TOP SERIES:

  1. Breaking Bad

    Synopsis: Breaking Bad chronicles the transformation of Walter White, a chemistry teacher with cancer, into a ruthless methamphetamine manufacturer, along with his former student Jesse Pinkman. What begins as a desperate attempt to secure his family's future turns into a spiral of power, violence and corruption.

  2. Black Clover

    Synopsis: Black Clover follows Asta, a young boy born without magical powers in a world where magic is everything. Despite his handicap, he dreams of becoming the Wizard King, facing powerful enemies and overcoming his limitations with sheer determination and strength.

  3. Loki

    Synopsis: Loki follows the adventures of the god of deception after the events of Avengers: Endgame. After escaping with the Tesseract, Loki is captured by the Temporal Variation Authority (TVA), an organization that protects the “sacred timeline.” Throughout the series, Loki confronts alternate realities, secrets of the multiverse and his own identity as he attempts to prevent universal chaos.

  4. The Umbrella Academy

    Synopsis: The Umbrella Academy follows a dysfunctional family of foster siblings with special abilities, raised by an enigmatic millionaire to save the world. After years of separation, they reunite after the death of their father and discover an apocalyptic threat. Throughout the series, the siblings face personal conflicts, time travel and dark secrets as they try to prevent the destruction of the planet.

  5. Narcos
    • Narcos: México

      Synopsis: Narcos: Mexico explores the origins of drug trafficking in Mexico, following the rise of Felix Gallardo, who unites the plazas to form the Guadalajara Cartel. In parallel, it shows the struggle of DEA agents, such as Kiki Camarena, as they attempt to dismantle the drug empire. The series reveals the growth of drug trafficking, violence, and corruption that marked the development of the illicit trade in the country.

    • Narcos: Colombia

      Synopsis: Narcos: Colombia follows the life of infamous drug trafficker Pablo Escobar and the rise of the Medellin Cartel in Colombia during the 1980s and 1990s. The series explores the war against drug trafficking, showing the devastating impact of cocaine trafficking in Colombia and the struggle of the authorities, especially the DEA, to capture Escobar and dismantle his empire.
